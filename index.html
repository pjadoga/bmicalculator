Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

Use the tool below to compute your BMI.

  • Enter your weight and height using standard or metric measures.
  • Select "Compute BMI" and your BMI will appear below.
Body Mass Index (BMI) is equal to Weight in Kilogram (Kg) divided by the square of Height in Meter(M2)
BMI=Kg/M2

BMI Categories to note:

Underweight = < 18.5

Normal weight = 18.5–24.9

Overweight = 25–29.9

Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater

BMI Table:
BMI value Interpretation Recommendation
Less or equals 18.5 Underweight Consume more calories
18.5 to 24.9 Normal weight Maintain good diet
25 to 29.9 Overweight Consider burning calories
30 and above Obesity Take drastic actions in calories burning
